Sound design and audio post-production for the official opening trailer of Young Con, Yandex's premier IT and career festival. This high-energy video was utilized to launch the main stage program and set the tone for an audience exceeding 10,000 attendees.
The focus of the sound design was to enhance the visual momentum and emphasize the scale and innovation of the tech conference. The project involved direct collaboration with the talented Yandex creative team to ensure seamless integration of sound and visual storytelling.
